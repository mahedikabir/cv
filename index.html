About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Media & Communication at Texas Tech University studying how large-scale scientific organizations adapt, collaborate, and maintain resilience through cyberinfrastructure and communication systems. My research focuses on organizational communication, Communities of Practice, risk mitigation, and the social dynamics of scientific work.

I also publish on digital activism, global communication, mental health communication, and computational analyses of media data. My academic journey is grounded in both professional journalism and strategic communication, which motivates my interdisciplinary approach to research and teaching.
